Why Animal Liberation?

Individual nonhuman animals, because they are endowed with a brain and nervous sytem, have conscious experience, subjective awareness, and the capacity to feel pain, love, fear, and many other complex emotions. Animals possesssentience, and as such, have an interest in their own lives that makes it unethical for humans to intentionally use, exploit, or kill them for our needs when we don't need to.
Because we, as individuals, cannot control what body we are born into, it is a logical extension of empathy, compassion, and the Golden Rule that animals should be allowed to enjoy their lives, families, and freedom in peace, just as we would if we were in their position.

Who We Are:

We are local Santa Barbara volunteers and activists that believe societal progress requires action, outreach, and organizing. We support all social justice movements, past and present.
We are all former meat-eaters who had a change in conscience upon being made aware of the truth of animal agriculture, and the horror of what its victims experience, and we are driven to bring an end to it within one generation.
